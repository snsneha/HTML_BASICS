ABOUT SARATHAS Going from Madras state to Tamil Nadu earlier in the year, the historic city of Tiruchirappalli saw the birth of one of its finest modern monuments in the Summer of ’69. It was the emergence of the largest textile showroom in India - Trichy Saratha’s.Founded on the 5th of August 1969 in one of the busiest roads not only in the city but also the entire state – the Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Road. The showroom is also in close proximity to the Rockfort Temple – an architectural marvel of the south and a major tourist attraction in the city.
